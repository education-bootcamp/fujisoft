Technology That Moves You Forward

Established in 2019, Fujisoft (PVT) LTD is an international technology company with a presence in both Japan and Sri Lanka. Headquartered in Sakado City, Saitama Prefecture, Japan, and operating locally from Panadura, Sri Lanka, we specialize in delivering high-performance software solutions across industries including finance, healthcare, logistics, and retail. With expertise in AI, cloud services, mobile development, and enterprise systems, our mission is to empower businesses through scalable, reliable, and future-ready technology. At Fujisoft, we blend global experience with local insight to create meaningful digital transformation.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, technologies, and process.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in web development, mobile app development, AI/ML integration, and custom software solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does it take to develop a project?

The timeline depends on the complexity and scope. Small projects take a few weeks, while larger solutions may take several months.

Can I request modifications after the project is completed?

Yes! We provide post-launch support and maintenance. Any additional features can be discussed separately.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages and technical support to ensure the smooth operation of your software.

Can you integrate third-party APIs into my project?

Absolutely! We specialize in API integrations, whether it's payment gateways, CRM systems, or AI-powered solutions.

How do you ensure data security in your projects?

We follow industry best practices, including encryption, secure authentication, and compliance standards like GDPR & ISO 27001.
